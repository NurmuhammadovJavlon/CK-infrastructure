Usually on each building site you can find a wide range of construction machines, which makes construction work easier, safer and faster. Depending on the application, construction machines are divided into various categories, which we discuss here.

Construction work can be divided into 3 main groups:

  • Earthworks
  • Road Works
  • Lifting

Digging Machines

Excavators: Excavators are construction and engineering works associated with the movement or movement of large amounts of soil or unformed rock. The two most popular types of construction equipment used in excavators are digging machines and surface digging machines. Excavators are the most popular and widely used types of heavy equipment, which are mainly used for digging holes and building pits. Different design options are available.

Loaders: Loaders machines are another type of digging machines that is very fast compared to excavators. The main purpose of the loader is to move loose soil.

Surface Digging Machines

Dozer: The surface digging machines are used to prepare the surface of a structure by moving and changing the soil. Bulldozer or dozer is an important excavation machine that pushes and squeezes out the soil to create a somewhat flat surface. This flat surface can be further aligned using graders.

Grader: A grader as a construction machine is popular because it can very effectively perform leveling jobs very effectively, creating a smooth surface for construction. This is especially useful for road construction projects.

Scraper: Scraper is another type of dredging machine used to remove a thin piece of soil. Next, the scraper moves the cleaned layer of soil to a distance of several meters. Scrapers are most widely used on large plots of land.

Road Works

Milling Machine: One of the main applications of construction machines is road and road construction. The road milling machine is used to process hard materials or to remove unwanted material from roads. It is widely used for repair jobs, so you can create a new layer by milling a broken layer. The concrete laying machine is an important construction machine used for laying asphalt or concrete on the roads.

Compactor: Compactors are used to compact concrete or asphalt on roadways. Compactors, such as earthwork or road-building machines, are used to compress soil and solid particles on construction sites and roads. They crush and stabilize the soil for further construction or lay asphalt or concrete roads to make them smooth and level roads.

Lifting Machinery: Tower Crane, Portal Crane, Cable Crane, Tractor Crane, Truck-Mounted Crane

Lifting machines, as the name implies, refer to lifting machines used to lift objects on construction sites. There are different types of lifting machines. For example: Tower cranes are used to lift concrete, steel, large tools and various building materials. They can be lifted to a great height and are a regular machine on all major construction sites.

Portal cranes can lift heavy loads and can be monitored mainly in storage areas and transshipment centers. There are cable cranes seen in the construction of dams and other construction sites that look like lines. These cranes can cover vast distances and deep valleys. Tractor cranes can move freely around the construction site. Trucks mounted cranes are used for lifting jobs of short duration. Their main advantage is that these cranes can drive on the road.
